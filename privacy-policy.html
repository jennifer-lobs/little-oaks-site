Privacy

Website Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Little Oaks Behavioral Services, LLC (“Little Oaks,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website, submit an inquiry, complete an online form, or otherwise interact with us online.

This policy does not replace our Notice of Privacy Practices. Protected health information maintained in connection with services is governed by HIPAA and our Notice of Privacy Practices.

Information we collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including names, contact information, a child’s information, insurance information, service needs, and other information submitted through forms or communications. We may also collect limited technical information such as browser type, device information, pages visited, date and time of visits, and referring website.

How we use information

  • Respond to inquiries and communicate about services.
  • Schedule consultations or appointments.
  • Review potential service eligibility and verify insurance information.
  • Operate, secure, and improve our website and services.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Cookies and analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies for functionality, security, and general website analytics. Browser settings may allow you to limit cookies, although some functions may not work as intended.

Sharing information

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share information with service providers that support our operations, when required by law, to protect safety or legal rights, or with authorization when required. Protected health information is disclosed only as permitted or required by applicable law.

Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. No electronic transmission or storage method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-party services

Our website may link to external websites or forms. Their privacy practices may differ from ours. Review the privacy terms of third-party services before submitting information.

Children’s privacy

This website is intended for parents, legal guardians, caregivers, and referral sources. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through the website.

Your choices

You may ask about, correct, or request deletion of information you submitted online where permitted by law. Some information may need to be retained for legal, clinical, or recordkeeping purposes.
