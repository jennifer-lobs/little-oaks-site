Our approach

Rooted in Care. Growing with Purpose.

Treatment plans start from the individual — skills, family priorities, and the settings where a child spends their day — rather than a fixed curriculum applied to every case.

One clinician, every plan

The Clinical Director personally reviews assessment data and approves every treatment plan — no case moves forward without direct BCBA oversight.

Skills that generalize

Because we work across school, home, and telehealth, goals are built to transfer between settings instead of staying confined to a therapy room.

Families as partners

Caregiver training is built into every plan, so progress continues between sessions and parents understand exactly what's being taught and why.

Services

Care delivered where children already are

The same clinical plan follows the child across settings, with one team coordinating the whole picture.

School-based support

On-site collaboration with classroom staff and IEP teams to build behavior support plans that hold up in a group setting.

In-home therapy

1:1 direct sessions and caregiver coaching in the family's natural environment, where new skills need to hold up day to day.

Telehealth

Video-based supervision, caregiver training, and team meetings that extend clinical reach and cut down on travel barriers.
